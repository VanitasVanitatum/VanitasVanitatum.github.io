For this document to display properly you need to use a monospace font like Consolas.

Combine elements to make spells. Choose to be mele, support, or dps. Combine any spell layout you want! Keep in mind, the hero you choose will still have a fighter/support/spellcaster role even though everyone's primary stat is agility.
The simplest character type is Melee or ranged character with Aura Trueshot, Aura Command and Passive Critical Strike
More complicated will be spellcaster for DPS.
Hardest will be support (heals) buffs/debuffs and summons.
